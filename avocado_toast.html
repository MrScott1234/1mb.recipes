Avocado Toast

https://1mb.recipes/avocado_toast.html

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread (ideally thick-sliced whole-grain bread)
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of garlic powder (optional)

Directions

  1. Toast your slice of bread until golden and firm.
  2. Remove the pit from your avocado. Use a big spoon to scoop out the flesh. Put it in a bowl and mash it up with a fork until it's as smooth as you like it. Mix in a pinch of salt (about ⅛ teaspoon) and add more to taste, and a pinch of garlic powder, if desired.
  3. Spread avocado on top of your toast.
