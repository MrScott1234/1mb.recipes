About 1MB.Recipes

Welcome to 1MB.Recipes!

As someone who is learning how to cook, I often had to deal with very sluggish recipe websites, where the actual content I am looking for is buried underneath 5 paragraphs of backstory, and 15 popups to subscribe to someone's mailing list.

I created this website as a hub for me to publish recipes that load quickly, have no javascript frontend bloat, and use less than 1 Megabyte of bandwidth, hence the name.

How does 1MB.Recipes work?

Below this section you'll find the index, which lists every recipe available on the website.

Simply click one you think looks interesting and get started, it's that easy!

Every recipe fits on one page, and is printer friendly.
